Help & Guide

PID Tuning

  • Kp (Prop): Main heating force. High = Fast, Low = Smooth. Too high causes overshoot.
  • Ki (Int): Precision. Fixes small temp gaps. Too high causes oscillation.
  • Kd (Deriv): Dampening. Resists change to stop overshoot. Too high causes jitter.
  • I-Band: Anti-Windup. Ki is only active within +/- this °C of target.
  • D-Filt: Noise Filter (0.0-1.0). 1.0=Off, 0.1=Strong. Rec: 0.1-0.2 for DS18B20.

Profile Steps

  • Ramp (Time): Reach target temp over specified Time (sec).
  • Soak (Time): Hold target temp for specified Time (sec).
  • Ramp (Rate): Reach target at Rate (deg/min).
  • Hold (Stable): Hold until temp change < Rate (deg/min).
  • Wait (Input): Wait for Pin # to match State (0 or 1).
